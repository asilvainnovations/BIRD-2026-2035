Data Privacy Notice (RA 10173 Compliance)

In compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), we are committed to protecting your personal information. By participating in this survey, you acknowledge and consent to the following:

  • Purpose Limitation: Your responses will be used solely for validating the BIRD 2026-2035 Investment Roadmap and improving strategic planning for BARMM.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the information necessary for analysis (demographics, sector, expertise, and survey responses).
  • Confidentiality: All responses will be anonymized in aggregate reports. Individual responses will not be disclosed without your explicit consent.
  • Security: Your data is encrypted during transmission and stored securely in compliance with industry standards.
  • Data Retention: Survey data will be retained for 5 years for longitudinal analysis, after which it will be securely deleted.
  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data by contacting privacy@mtit.bangsamoro.gov.ph.
